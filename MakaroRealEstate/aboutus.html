Let us help you.

Makaro's Realty is a real estate marketplace and information hub in British Columbia and Ontario. Whether you're buying, selling, learning, or simply perusing, Makaro provides you with a fast and easy home search experience, and arms you with the information and connections you need to take on your real estate goals.

Launched in 2019, our goal at Makaro is to be the #1 source for real estate search across Canada, and the industry leader in the evolution of home search behaviour.

Our leadership team brings our vision to life.

OUR TEAM

Who we are in nutshell

President

- Marinelle Reyes

As President of Makaro, Nelle oversees Makaro as well as the company's portfolio of business information units that include environmental, property and financial information. Nelle also leads Makaros efforts in acquisitions, divestitures and partnerships. A graduate of the University of the Philippines, Nelle holds two masters degrees from Harvard University: an MBA and an MPA.

Vice President

- Kaan Akaydin

As General Manager, Kaan leads the strategy and business operations at Makaro. With over 15 years of real estate tech experience, he is responsible for growing the evolution of home search with REW and driving value to both sides of the transaction. Prior to launching Makaro, Kaan held various product roles with Top Producer and Realtor.com, building products and services for some of the largest real estate brands in Toronto. Kaan was also part of the initial team that launched iHome Web Solutions, the first fully customizable agent website with national ABC coverage.

Director, Sales

- Rodrigo Paim

Rodrigo is responsible for developing customer growth and acquisition strategies in British Columbia and Ontario, across Makaro’s verticals. With over 20 years of sales and management experience, Rodrigo leads Makaro’s east coast sales team to provide the right solutions to our industry partners. Prior to Makaro, Rodrigo was with Travelzoo as the Senior Sales Director for Canada, recognized for consistently meeting revenue goals, and developing training programs for all North American sales teams. Rodrigo went to school for Music and creative writing, but one day stumbled into sales and never turned back.
